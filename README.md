# Compassor

Say goodbye to the hassle of spinning your map around to find your direction! Compassor displays your destination directly on an intuitive radar screen. No matter how the pointer turns, your target's bearing is clear at a glance—bringing navigation back to pure instinct. Whether you're navigating the city or exploring the outdoors, you'll never be lost again.

An Android application for mapping and navigation with waypoint and route management.

## Features

- **Search:** Search for locations and see a list of results prioritized by distance. You can set a search result as your destination or save it as a waypoint.
- **Waypoints:**
    - Save your current location as a waypoint.
    - Long-press on the map to create a new waypoint at that location.
    - Click on a waypoint's marker to edit its name or delete it.
- **Routes:**
    - Create routes by selecting from your saved waypoints.
    - Edit existing routes to change their name or the waypoints they contain.
    - Delete routes you no longer need.
    - The selected route is drawn on the map as a blue line.

# Compassor (中文简介)

告别在地图上来回旋转找方向的困扰！Compassor 将目的地直接呈现在一个直观的雷达屏幕上。无论指针如何转动，目标方位一目了然——让导航回归最纯粹的直觉。城市穿行，户外探索，从此不再迷路。

一款安卓地图导航应用，提供路点和路线管理功能。

## 功能

- **搜索:** 搜索地点并查看按距离优先排序的结果列表。您可以将搜索结果设置为目的地或保存为路点。
- **路点:**
    - 将您当前的位置保存为路点。
    - 在地图上长按以在指定位置创建一个新的路点。
    - 点击路点的地图标记以编辑其名称或将其删除。
- **路线:**
    - 从您保存的路点中选择，以创建路线。
    - 编辑现有路线以更改其名称或包含的路点。
    - 删除您不再需要的路线。
    - 您创建的路线将在地图上以蓝色线条显示。
